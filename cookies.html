Cookie Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026
In short: Bidalize uses only the cookies it needs to sign you in and keep the app working. We don't use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies.

This Cookie Policy explains how Bidalize (Vorapure UK Ltd) uses cookies and similar technologies on bidalize.com. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They let the site remember your actions and preferences — for example, keeping you signed in as you move between pages.

The cookies we use

We keep this deliberately minimal. We use strictly necessary cookies to run the Service, and we do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

TypePurposeNeeds consent?
Authentication / sessionKeeps you securely signed in and maintains your session while you use the app. Set by our authentication provider (Supabase).No — strictly necessary
SecurityHelps protect against fraudulent or unauthorised activity.No — strictly necessary
PreferencesRemembers basic in-app choices so the interface behaves as you expect.No — strictly necessary

Because we currently use only strictly necessary cookies, they are exempt from the consent requirement under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR). If we introduce analytics or other non-essential cookies in future, we will ask for your consent first and update this policy.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Blocking strictly necessary cookies will stop you from signing in and using the app, since they are required for it to function.

Changes

We will update this policy if our use of cookies changes, and post the new version here.
