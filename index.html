How it works

From a pile of quotes to a signed-off decision.

The messy part of tail-spend buying is that every supplier answers differently — currency, tax, delivery wording, warranty terms. Bidalize does the reconciling.

01

Drop in the quotes

Forward the PDFs and photos you already get. Bidalize reads them — even mixed languages and Urdu numerals.

02

Everything, one basis

Prices, tax, delivery and warranty normalised so you compare like with like — not guessing.

03

Scored & ranked

Set what matters. Every quote ranked with the reasoning shown — plus where to negotiate harder.

04

Send for sign-off

One link to your approver. They decide with a one-time code — no login, no seat. All logged.
